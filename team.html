    О Team Liquid

    Team Liquid is a professional team, founded in the Netherlands in 2000. Originally a Brood War clan, the team switched to SC2 during the SC2 Beta in 2010, and became one of the most successful foreign teams. On December 6, 2012, it was announced that the team had formed a Dota 2 division.
